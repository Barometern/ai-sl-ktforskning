# Brick Wall Workflow

## Purpose

When evidence is missing, contradictory, or weak, the framework should shift from direct conclusion seeking to structured problem decomposition.

## Scope

This workflow applies to research impasses rather than routine document review.

## Audience

- Genealogists
- Workflow designers

- Research plan template: [`/templates/research-plan.md`](/Users/ludvigrogestam/Documents/Codex/ai-sl-ktforskning/templates/research-plan.md)

## Workflow Stages

1. Summarize the current claim and unresolved conflict.
2. Identify missing source classes and untested hypotheses.
3. Separate data absence from reasoning uncertainty.
4. Propose targeted next actions with expected payoff.
