# Research Report Template

## Purpose

This template defines the canonical shape of a research report so outputs remain reviewable and comparable across workflows and implementations.

## Scope

It covers report structure, not content generation logic.

## Audience

- Genealogists
- Workflow designers

## Dependencies

Workflow context is defined in [`/workflows/research-workflow.md`](../workflows/research-workflow.md).

## Related Documents

- Research plan: [`/templates/research-plan.md`](research-plan.md)
- Conflict report: [`/templates/conflict-report.md`](conflict-report.md)

## Template Sections

- research question
- scope and assumptions
- sources reviewed
- findings
- evidence summary
- conflicts and limitations
- next steps
