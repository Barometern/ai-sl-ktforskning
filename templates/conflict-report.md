# Conflict Report Template

## Purpose

This template ensures conflicts are documented as first-class research artifacts rather than buried inside narrative prose.

## Scope

It applies to contradictions between records, interpretations, or identity hypotheses.

## Audience

- Genealogists
- Reasoning designers

## Dependencies

Conflict policy is defined in [`/specs/conflict-resolution.md`](../specs/conflict-resolution.md).

## Related Documents

- Compare sources prompt: [`/prompts/compare-sources.md`](../prompts/compare-sources.md)
- Research report: [`/templates/research-report.md`](research-report.md)

## Template Sections

- disputed claim
- conflicting observations
- source quality comparison
- current confidence posture
- proposed resolution path
