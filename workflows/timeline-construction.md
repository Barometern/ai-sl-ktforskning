# Timeline Construction Workflow

## Purpose

Timelines convert distributed evidence into a chronological model that exposes sequence, movement, and gaps in the record.

## Scope

This workflow covers temporal ordering and annotation, not final narrative writing.

## Audience

- Workflow designers
- Template authors

## Dependencies

Evidence and geography modules provide the interpretive context.

## Related Documents

- Timeline template: [`/templates/timeline.md`](../templates/timeline.md)
- Person summary template: [`/templates/person-summary.md`](../templates/person-summary.md)

## Workflow Stages

1. Normalize event dates and date ranges.
2. Attach location context and provenance.
3. Distinguish observed events from inferred intervals.
4. Flag unexplained gaps and reversals.
